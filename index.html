Why IRIS?

The International Rural School Report system is state of the art technology integration that school administrators rely upon world wide to can login and create, read, update and delete information on the condition of the school including general issues and the condition of equipment devices in the school (TVs, tablets, chromebooks, servers, routers, HDMI cables). A board member can log in and mark the action as done, scheduled, or ignored, and comment with more information.
